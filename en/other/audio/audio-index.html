Weight of the World

This is an immensely personal project which I've been working on this year. It isn't music - not really. If you've heard of "stream-of-consciousness" type writing, then you can imagine this as the musical equivalent. Each track is trying to say something, but only I know what it means. Of course, that doesn't mean your interpretation is less valid. The meaning is fluid. It changes on each listen.

Of course, I am not musically competent, so this is all sample-based. Hundreds of samples distorted, looped, misremembered, deluded. If any copyright holders object to my use of their samples, contact me on the Contact page on this site for swift action.

Kattjakt (Abandoned Beta)

Date: 2018-03-16

The unfinished sequel to Coconuts on Earth. It shows improvement design-wise, but a lacking in many other important areas, which ultimately led to it never being completed. It cuts off rather suddenly after reaching a nature preserve. I once again cannot recommend this game. It is more playable than those that came before it, but that's like saying a broken leg is better than a broken arm. It's still broken, and at the end of the day, you want neither.

Coconuts on Earth

Date: 2017-11-10

A sequel/upgrade to Coconuts in Space, except after the events of the first game, the coconuts are on earth. How exciting!... or maybe not.

This is, like its predecessor, very far from good. This time, there are even more horrible assets in the mix, and a "story," complete with awful cutscenes. Once again, this was basically just a way for my very young self to learn the engine's fundamentals. It's not playable by any means.

Coconuts in Space

Date: 2017-05-08

I was 12 and I had just barely started figuring out Unity when I made this. While technically my second game on the engine, I consider it the only one that is playable (not to be confused with 'good'. it is far from that.) En contraire, it is borderline uncontrollable, and a hodge-podge of random assets.

The premise? You're a coconut, located on a space station of some kind. Your goal is to leave. Other coconuts are trying to stop you. That is all.
